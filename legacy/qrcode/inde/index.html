Our Core Values at Silverleaf Academy

Lead the Way

We embody the spirit of servant leadership by consistently practicing empathy, prioritizing the needs of others and setting a powerful example through your actions. We inspire those around us by uplifting our peers, modeling integrity and creating a culture where everyone is encouraged to reach their full potential.

Speak, Listen and Learn

We foster a culture of open, respectful and thoughtful communication. We work together by expressing our ideas clearly, listening actively to others and embracing diverse perspectives. Through meaningful dialogue and a willingness to learn from one another, we continuously grow as individuals and as a community.

Unwavering Mission Focus

We set ambitious goals and pursue them with discipline, determination and resilience. We hold ourselves and each other to the highest standards, never losing sight of our purpose—even in the face of obstacles. Our commitment to excellence and our mission drives us to achieve greatness together.

Ask Why, Why Not

We think critically, question assumptions and seek evidence in every decision we make. We are proactive and innovative, always exploring what is possible and finding creative solutions that drive positive change. We believe curiosity and courage are the keys to unlocking new opportunities for our students and ourselves.

Family

We build a community where every individual feels a genuine sense of belonging, care and support. We treat each other with respect and kindness, celebrate our achievements together, and stand united through challenges. Like a true family, we nurture trust and connection, ensuring everyone feels valued, seen and safe.

Fun (Love)

We infuse our school with joy, positivity and heartfelt connection. We believe that learning and working should be enjoyable and that laughter, creativity and genuine acts of kindness make our environment vibrant and motivating.

Faith

At Silverleaf Academy, "Faith" is about nurturing trust, wellness, and conscious awareness in ourselves and our community. We believe in our shared mission and in each other's potential. Faith means being mindful and present, supporting each other's mental, emotional, and spiritual wellbeing, and persevering with optimism and resilience. Through self-awareness and collective belief, we strive to create a supportive and inclusive environment for all.
